About App

You’ve done it. You’ve arrived at your destination. You have responsibilities to take care of, obligations with others, and a wealth of other things to do before you relax at the end of the day—as the clock continues to tick the minutes away.

But right as you arrive—you encounter a problem you face every single day: where in the world do you park? And if you find a spot, it’s going to cost you an arm and a leg.

A team of out of the box thinkers, developers and engineers have joined forces to develop a solution for an everyday problem that most drivers face in the form of an innovate app named Pwayz. Driveway is an app that lets drivers connect up to local homeowners to get permission to block driveway, park in their driveway/garage or any other private parking.

While other services simply allow space-owners to advertise, Pwayz puts drivers and space-owners in direct contact with each other—aided by live-routing that follows real-time traffic and road data; so drivers can park fast and move on with their days, and space-owners can get paid.

How it Works?

Drivers

Location

Find the location that works for you and pre-book your space to avoid disappointments.

Hassle free

No coins needed and no more parking tickets. All payments are made right through the app via credit/debit card, Apple pay or Android pay.

Save time

Have you found yourself wasting time circling the block over and over looking for parking? The Pwayz app shows you nearest parking spaces on the map and in a list format.

Spaceowners

Driveway unused while you go to work or do you have extra space?

List your underutilised space on the Pwayz app and make an additional earning by renting out your space to fellow citizens.

Set parking availability

Control your availability with the in-app calendar. Turn on Auto-approval and let the Pwayz app take care of the rest.

Get paid

Securely add your bank details to the Pwayz app and transfer your earnings to your bank account. You keep 100% of your earnings.
