Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why do I need to upload a scan or photo of my passport or driver's license?

We use Stripe, to handle all our payments. Stripe is a global online payment processor that handles billions of dollars every year.

The reason they require such information is due to stringent identity regulations from their banking partners in order to verify the legitimacy of Stripe's users and businesses. This is known as "Know Your Customer" (KYC), and consists of both the collection of all required information and then verifying this -- you can read more on this here -https://stripe.com/docs/connect/identity-verification

Is providing Identification to Stripe secure?

Stripe is trusted is trusted by thousands of businesses across the globe including; Facebook, Delivery, and ASOS.

Stripe has been audited by a PCI-certified auditor and is certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1. This is the most stringent level of certification available in the payments industry. To accomplish this, they make use of best-in-class security tools and practices to maintain a high level of security at Stripe.

You can read more about the security level at Stripe here - https://stripe.com/docs/security/stripe

What are the benefits of using Stripe as a customer?

We have rigorously researched the market and concluded that Stripe offers the best withdrawal fees.

Other payment gateways charge you percentage fees exceeding 3% for all withdrawals and only allow you to withdraw your earnings once they are over £20

Stripe charges only £0.10 per payout regardless of the amount. So whether you are withdrawing £1 or £1000 the payout fee is £0.10
